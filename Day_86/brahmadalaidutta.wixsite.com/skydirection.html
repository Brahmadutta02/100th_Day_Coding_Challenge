    ABOUT SKYDIRECTION

    The sky can be an important consideration for travel agencies, especially for activities such as flight booking. Airlines choose flight routes based on wind patterns, fuel efficiency, and safety considerations. Understanding the direction of the sky can be crucial for flight planning.  Incorporating these sky-related factors into a travel agency's offerings can enhance the travel experience for customers.
